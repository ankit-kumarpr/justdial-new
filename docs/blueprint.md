# **App Name**: LocalConnect

## Core Features:

- Business Search: Search for businesses by name or category.
- Detailed Profiles: View business profiles with contact information, addresses, and ratings.
- One-Tap Calling: Call businesses directly from their profile.
- Review Submission: Allow users to submit reviews and ratings for businesses. 
- AI-Powered Recommendation: The tool analyzes user behavior to provide smart and relevant business suggestions, driving more customers to the businesses.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant yellow (#FFDA63) for main accents and highlights.
- Background color: Light yellow (#FAF4E6), very desaturated.
- Accent color: Deep blue (#3854A3) to complement the vibrant yellow, for a dynamic feel. The deep blue is on the opposite side of the color wheel from yellow.
- Font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, suitable for headlines or body text.
- Use clear and recognizable icons for business categories and actions.
- Clean and intuitive layout with clear separation of information blocks.
- Subtle transitions and animations for user interactions, e.g., when searching or opening a business profile.
